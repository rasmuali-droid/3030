All-Time Greats
Giorgio Petrosyan: Widely considered the best technician in history.
Buakaw Banchamek: A Muay Thai and kickboxing icon known for his immense power and global popularity.
Ernesto Hoost: Four-time K-1 World Grand Prix Champion.
Ramon Dekkers: A legendary Dutch fighter who conquered Muay Thai in Thailand.
Semmy Schilt: A dominant heavyweight with multiple K-1 and Glory titles.
Peter Aerts: "The Dutch Lumberjack," known for his longevity and knockout power.
Don "The Dragon" Wilson: Renowned for winning 11 world titles across 3 weight divisions.
