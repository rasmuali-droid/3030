The Flat Earth theory is a largely 21st-century conspiracy theory and archaic, scientifically disproven belief that the Earth is a flat plane or disk rather than an oblate spheroid. It posits that scientific evidence, such as satellite imagery, is fabricated and that the Earth is surrounded by an ice wall, rejecting centuries of established astronomical and geographical knowledge.

Key Aspects of Flat Earth Theory:
Model: Proponents often believe the Earth is a stationary disk with the North Pole at the center and a massive,150-foot tall ice wall—Antarctica—around the circumference to hold the oceans in.
Conspiracy Theory: The theory hinges on the belief that NASA and other government agencies are faking photos of the Earth from space and perpetrating a global cover-up.
Scientific Disproof: The theory contradicts fundamental physics and direct observation, including the way ships sail over the horizon, the curvature of the Earth visible from high altitudes, and the existence of time zones.
Modern Resurgence: While historically, most civilizations knew the Earth was spherical, the modern flat Earth movement has gained traction through social media and online conspiracy groups.
